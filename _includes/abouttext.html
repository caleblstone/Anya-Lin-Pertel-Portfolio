Anya Lin Pertel is a painter and printmaker based in New York City. She received her BFA from the School of Visual Arts in 2023.

Her work explores the relationship between nature and the human experience, often using vibrant colors and abstract forms to convey emotion and movement.

In addition to her studio practice, Pertel has exhibited her work in various galleries and art fairs, including the Armory Show and Frieze New York.
